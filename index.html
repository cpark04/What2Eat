  what2Eat is a website where you can input ingredients that you would like to use in your next meal and generate a random recipe. This site was mainly built so you can help minimize waste and create meals with what you have.

  For just a basic search, start typing in the ingredients in the search bar and click on the item from the drop down. Keep adding in ingredients that you would like included in your recipes. Once you have entered in all the ingredients, click on the 'Search for Recipe' button to generate a recipe. Please note that there may be combinations of ingredients that will not have recipes. If by chance the recipe that was generated is not to your taste, you may close the popped up window and just click 'Search for Recipe' once again until you find a recipe you like.

  For a more complex search, click on the 'Need More Options?' button. You will be presented with a list of different parameters you can input. These filter options were added to accomodate you if you have any dietary restrictions. If you would like to just go back to the basic search, click the 'Need More Options?' button to close the filter page.
